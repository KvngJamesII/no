Firebase Authentication Setup Guide

1. Go to your Firebase Console: https://console.firebase.google.com/
2. Select your "soccerx" project
3. In the left menu, click on "Authentication"
4. Click on the "Sign-in method" tab
5. Enable the "Email/Password" provider (click on it and toggle the "Enable" switch)
6. Save the changes

Next, ensure your app's domains are authorized:

1. Still in Authentication, click on the "Settings" tab
2. Find the "Authorized domains" section
3. Add your Replit domain (e.g., "f4bc0a0e-69d7-4ab3-974e-071c60bf8409-00-2uwms339hn8qq.riker.replit.dev")
4. Save the changes

This will allow the sign-up and login processes to work correctly with your application.
